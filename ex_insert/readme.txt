Vanilla (0-1046)
    These slots are used by vanilla DKC 2 and will never be allocated publicly to anyone, not even ex developers.

Reserved (1048-1FFE)
    These slots may be used by the ex developers in the future and will never be allocated publicly to anyone else.

Private (2000-2FFE)
    These slots will never be publicly allocated to anyone however any individual can use them privately in their own hacks. Please note that publicly releasing sprites in this ID range is highly discouraged because this range is unregulated. ID conflicts between other individuals may occur. Such ID conflicts will not be resolved or enforced by the ex developers.

Public (3000-7FFE)
    These slots are regulated by the ex developers and will be officially allocated to any individual upon submission/approval of an ID request. Using IDs in this range without a public allocation approval is highly frowned upon as it hurts the overall health and cross-compatibility of the community. The ex developers will enforce and uphold the valid use of these IDs.
